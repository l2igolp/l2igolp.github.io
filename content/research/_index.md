L2I – Laboratório de Lasers Intensos, Laboratory for Intense Lasers – is a research infrastructure for high-power, ultrafast lasers and applications. L2I is located in the Alameda Campus of Instituto Superior Tecnico, Lisbon and is a member of the <a href="https://www.fct.pt/en/financiamento/programas-de-financiamento/infraestruturas-de-investigacao/">Portuguese Roadmap of Strategic Research Infrastructures</a> under the <a href="https://www.ipfn.tecnico.ulisboa.pt/llpt/">Laserlab-Portugal</a> distributed infrastructure.

Our research is performed through the development and application of custom ultrafast laser sources from the near- and mid-IR, and covers the following topics:

<ul>
<li>Laser science and technology</li>
<li>Ultrafast pulse diagnostics</li>
<li>Nonlinear optical phenomena</li>
<li>Ultrafast laser ablation</li>
<li>Nonlinear material characterization</li>
<li>Two-photon excited fluorescence</li>
<li>Time-resolved infrared spectroscopy (TRIR)</li>
<li>Laser Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)</li>
</ul>

L2I also has an important role in:
<ul>
<li>Training of young researchers, through PhD and MSc theses, BSc Graduation Projects and Research Internships.</li>
<li>Testing and preparation of experiments to be carried out at large-scale facilities</li>
</ul>
